DS Unit 4 Sprint 16 - Technical Preparation & Career Readiness

Welcome to DS Unit 4 Sprint 16

Congratulations on reaching the final sprint of the Data Science program! This sprint serves as a capstone to your technical education by focusing on two critical areas for your career success: technical interview preparation and professional branding.

In the technical preparation modules, you'll work through advanced coding challenges similar to those you'll encounter in technical interviews and on the General Code Assessment (GCA). For career readiness, you'll develop a polished LinkedIn profile and resume that effectively showcase your skills and projects to potential employers.

Tips for Success in this Sprint

  • Practice coding challenges daily, even beyond the assigned exercises
  • Schedule regular mock technical interviews with peers
  • Take the practice GCA tests to identify areas for improvement
  • Get feedback on your LinkedIn profile and resume from peers and instructors
  • Connect with at least 5-10 industry professionals each week
  • Attend all Code-Alongs for additional practice with complex problems
  • Review your projects from previous sprints to include in your resume
