Module Overview

In this module, you will complete your LinkedIn profile and create a professional resume that effectively showcases your skills and experiences to potential employers. You'll learn how to tailor your resume to specific job postings and present yourself as a compelling candidate.

Resume Best Practices

A strong technical resume should include the following elements:

  • Contact Information: Your name, email, phone number, location, LinkedIn URL, and GitHub URL
  • Professional Summary: A brief overview of your technical skills and career goals
  • Technical Skills: List of programming languages, frameworks, tools, and methodologies you're proficient in
  • Projects: Descriptions of relevant technical projects, highlighting your contributions and the technologies used
  • Experience: Work history with emphasis on technical responsibilities and achievements
  • Education: Your educational background, including your BloomTech training

Optimizing your LinkedIn profile for maximum visibility to recruiters.

Resume vs. LinkedIn Profile

While your resume and LinkedIn profile should be aligned, there are important differences:

  • Resume: Typically 1-2 pages, tailored for specific roles, formal language, focused content
  • LinkedIn: More comprehensive, includes recommendations and endorsements, can showcase media, conversational tone

Your LinkedIn profile should expand on your resume, providing more detail about your projects, skills, and professional interests.

Tailoring Your Resume

Analyzing Job Descriptions

When applying for jobs, it's essential to customize your resume for each position by:

  • Identifying key skills and requirements in the job posting
  • Highlighting relevant experience that matches those requirements
  • Using similar terminology to what's in the job description
  • Quantifying your achievements where possible (e.g., "Improved performance by 30%")
  • Prioritizing information that's most relevant to the specific role

ATS Optimization

Many companies use Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) to screen resumes. To ensure your resume gets through:

  • Use a clean, simple format without complex design elements
  • Include relevant keywords from the job description
  • Avoid using headers, footers, or text boxes as ATS may not read them
  • Use standard section headings (e.g., "Experience" rather than "Career History")
  • Submit files in .docx or .pdf format
