Welcome to ACS 4!

In this sprint, you will continue to improve upon your programming and problem-solving skills by completing a series of advanced challenges. In addition use this time to ensure that you have a good handle on all the material you learned up to this point!

In this sprint, the career artifact focus is on building your LinkedIn profile.

Tips on how to succeed in this sprint:

  • Complete the advanced challenges
  • Attend ACS Live Classes
  • Take the official General Code Assessment (GCA)
  • Build your LinkedIn profile
