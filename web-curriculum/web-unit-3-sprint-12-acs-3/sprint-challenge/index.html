Sprint Challenge Overview

The Sprint Challenge is your opportunity to demonstrate your understanding of the concepts covered in this sprint. You'll need to complete both technical and career readiness components.

Challenge Components

1. Technical Assessment

Complete the official GCA test to demonstrate your technical skills in:

  • Object manipulation and letter counting
  • Caching strategies
  • Object references and linked lists
  • Class implementation

2. Career Readiness

Submit your technical resume, which should include:

  • Updated technical skills
  • Project experience
  • Professional formatting
  • Relevant experience and education

  2. Submit your technical resume
