Module 4: Career Readiness - LinkedIn + Resume

Overview

Module 4 combines both LinkedIn profile development and technical resume building. You'll learn how to create a compelling online presence and a professional resume that effectively showcases your skills to potential employers.

Core Competencies

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Create a compelling LinkedIn profile
  • Build a professional technical resume
  • Identify relevant job opportunities
  • Network effectively with industry professionals

LinkedIn Profile Development

Profile Components

  • Professional headshot and banner
  • Compelling headline and summary
  • Detailed work experience
  • Technical skills and endorsements
  • Projects and portfolio
  • Education and certifications

Resume Building

Resume Sections

  • Contact information
  • Professional summary
  • Technical skills
  • Projects
  • Work experience
  • Education

Job Search Strategies

Finding Jobs

  • Identifying relevant job titles
  • Researching companies
  • Using job search platforms
  • Networking strategies
  • Application process

Practice Activities

Career Readiness Activities

  1. Update your LinkedIn profile with all recommended sections
  2. Create a technical resume following the provided template
  3. Research and identify 3 potential job opportunities
  4. Complete the career readiness quiz
