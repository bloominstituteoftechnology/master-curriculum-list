Code-Alongs

About Code-Alongs

Code-Alongs are live experiences taught by our expert instructors designed to prepare you for concepts found in the sprint challenges. Code-Alongs are your opportunity to work on complex job-ready problems in a live and engaging environment.

During Code-Alongs, you'll follow along with an instructor as they guide you through practical examples related to the technologies used in your Learn and Be Curious project. These sessions will help you apply theoretical concepts to real-world scenarios.

Code-Along Schedule

Code-Alongs are offered multiple times per week to accommodate different schedules. You can sign up for Code-Alongs through the BloomTech platform.

How to Register

  1. Log in to your BloomTech account
  2. Navigate to the "Live Events" section
  3. Select the Code-Along you want to attend
  4. Choose a time slot that works for you
  5. Click "Register" to secure your spot

Preparation Tips

  • Review related modules: Familiarize yourself with the relevant module content
  • Set up your environment: Have your development environment ready
  • Download starter code: Get the starter code repository for the session
  • Prepare questions: Note any specific questions you have about the topic
  • Take notes: Be ready to capture important concepts during the session
