Practice Activities

LinkedIn Profile Building Exercise

Follow these steps to build your LinkedIn profile:

  1. Create a LinkedIn account if you haven't already
  2. Add a professional headshot
  3. Write a compelling headline
  4. Complete your summary section
  5. Add your education and work experience
  6. List your technical skills
  7. Add your projects
  8. Get endorsements from connections
