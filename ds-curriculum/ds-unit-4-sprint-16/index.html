DS Unit 4 Sprint 16 - Technical Preparation & Career Readiness

Welcome to Sprint 16

Welcome to Sprint 16: Technical Preparation & Career Readiness! This sprint focuses on technical preparation through CodeSignal and General Code Assessment (GCA), along with career readiness through LinkedIn profile development and resume building.

Learning Objectives

By the end of this sprint, you should be able to:

  • Master CodeSignal platform and GCA preparation
  • Develop advanced coding challenges and algorithms
  • Build a professional LinkedIn profile
  • Create and optimize your technical resume
  • Prepare for technical interviews
