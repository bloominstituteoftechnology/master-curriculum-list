Workshops

About Workshops

Workshops are live interactive sessions designed to offer feedback and insights into your work as you develop your Learn and Be Curious project. These sessions are facilitated by instructors and are offered throughout the week to accommodate your schedule.

During workshops, you'll have the opportunity to collaborate with peers, receive guidance on your project, and overcome challenges you may be facing. These sessions are invaluable for refining your ideas and improving your implementation.

Workshop 1: Project Ideation

Brainstorm and develop your project idea with guidance from instructors and peers.

Key Activities

  • Collaborative brainstorming
  • Idea evaluation
  • Technical feasibility assessment
  • Scope definition

Workshop 2: Design Document Review

Get feedback on your design document before beginning implementation.

Key Activities

  • Design document walkthrough
  • API design critique
  • DynamoDB table design review
  • Scope adjustment

Workshop 3: Implementation Progress

Share your implementation progress and get help with technical challenges.

Key Activities

  • Code review
  • Debugging assistance
  • Architecture optimization
  • Feature prioritization

Workshop Schedule

Workshops are offered multiple times per week to accommodate different schedules. You can sign up for workshops through the BloomTech platform.

How to Register

  1. Log in to your BloomTech account
  2. Navigate to the "Live Events" section
  3. Select the workshop you want to attend
  4. Choose a time slot that works for you
  5. Click "Register" to secure your spot

Workshop Preparation Tips

  • Come prepared: Review the workshop description and complete any pre-work
  • Bring your questions: Make a list of specific questions or challenges you're facing
  • Be ready to share: Have your project materials ready to present
  • Participate actively: Engage with instructors and peers to get the most value
  • Take notes: Document feedback and action items to implement later
