Sprint Challenge

Project Setup

This challenge will test both your technical coding skills and your career readiness through LinkedIn profile development.

Sprint Challenge Objectives

  • Demonstrate mastery of array manipulation and looping techniques
  • Apply string operations to solve complex problems
  • Utilize boolean logic and conditional statements in problem-solving
  • Optimize your LinkedIn profile for job searching
  • Create effective outreach messages for networking
  • Successfully solve CodeSignal assessment problems

Setup Instructions

  1. Complete your LinkedIn profile based on the guidelines from Module 1 and Module 4
  2. Attempt the CodeSignal assessment to practice your technical interview skills
  3. Submit your work through the assessment platform

Part 1: Career Development

For the career development portion of this sprint challenge, you will need to:

  • Complete your LinkedIn profile with all required sections (Headline, About, Experience, Education, Skills)
  • Craft one warm outreach message for someone in your existing network
  • Draft one cold outreach message for a potential connection at a company of interest
  • Submit screenshots of your completed LinkedIn profile

LinkedIn Profile Checklist

  • Professional profile photo
  • Compelling headline with job title and key skills
  • About section that tells your professional story
  • Experience section with detailed descriptions
  • Education section including BloomTech
  • Skills section with at least 10 relevant skills
  • Customized LinkedIn URL
  • Featured projects/work with links or media

Part 2: Technical Assessment

For the technical assessment portion of this sprint challenge, you will need to:

  • Complete the CodeSignal assessment provided by your instructor
  • Solve algorithmic problems similar to those covered in the modules
  • Submit your solutions within the allotted time

Technical Assessment Preparation Tips

  • Review array manipulation and string operations
  • Practice time and space complexity analysis
  • Refresh your knowledge of loops and conditional statements
  • Review the code-along exercises from this sprint
  • Practice explaining your thought process as you solve problems
  • Read each problem carefully and identify edge cases
