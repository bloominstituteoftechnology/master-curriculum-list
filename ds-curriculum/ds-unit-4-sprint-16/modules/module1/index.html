Module 1: Career Readiness - LinkedIn

Overview

Module 1 focuses on creating a professional LinkedIn profile that highlights your technical skills and attracts recruiters. A well-crafted LinkedIn profile is essential for your job search and professional networking.

Core Competencies

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

  • Create a professional LinkedIn profile
  • Optimize your profile for recruiters
  • Highlight your technical skills and projects
  • Network effectively on LinkedIn

Build Your LinkedIn Profile

Key Components of a Strong LinkedIn Profile

  • Professional headshot
  • Compelling headline
  • Detailed summary
  • Work experience
  • Education
  • Skills and endorsements
  • Projects and portfolio

Practice Activities

LinkedIn Profile Building Exercise

Follow these steps to build your LinkedIn profile:

  1. Create a LinkedIn account if you haven't already
  2. Add a professional headshot
  3. Write a compelling headline
  4. Complete your summary section
  5. Add your education and work experience
  6. List your technical skills
  7. Add your projects
  8. Get endorsements from connections
